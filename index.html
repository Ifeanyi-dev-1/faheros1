Pure Water, Pure Commitment

FOSTER AU MULTIBIZ is a dynamic and customer-focused enterprise dedicated to the production and distribution of high-quality table water under the trusted brand name FAHEROESNO1. Headquartered at Faheroes Crescent, Off Odaa Opue Street, Gracious Estate, Nkwelle Ezunaka, Oyi L.G.A., Anambra State, Nigeria, we pride ourselves on delivering clean, safe, and refreshing drinking water to households and businesses alike.

Our Product

FAHEROESNO1 is a premium quality table water brand, hygienically packaged in 75cl bottles and produced under stringent quality control standards. Our water is treated, purified, and packaged to meet both national and international health regulations, ensuring safety and satisfaction for our consumers.

Quality Assurance

With NAFDAC Registration Number A1-107168, FOSTER AU MULTIBIZ operates in compliance with all required food safety and regulatory standards. Our facility employs modern water purification technology, and our staff are trained in hygienic handling and packaging procedures.

Mission

To provide safe, affordable, and accessible drinking water that promotes public health and wellness.

Vision

To be a leading brand in Nigeria’s bottled water industry, known for quality, integrity, and innovation.
